Nice To Meet You

I’m Christoph. I’ve been listening to, writing and playing music all my life. About 10 years ago I opened The Lullabye Factory, an Analog Recording Studio in Amsterdam. The Lullabye Factory belongs to the very few studios that can still record, mix and master a record completely analog. A few years later when I started Tape Your Mix, a website where I offer all kinds of Tape services for those who cannot come to the studio, I got into Mastering. I started to really like Mastering and got a lot of positive feedback on the Analog Focus in my Sound Workflow. With VIBE Audio Mastering my goal is to create polished professional sounding masters, with a focus on Tape, Tubes & Transformers. With the ‘The Gear You Want To Hear’ option you can pick the analog tools that I then will use to master your work!
